The One-Question Test

Ask this every time someone says "agent"

"Does it think & generate, or just follow fixed steps?"

Fixed steps?

Washing machine.
Relax. Test the recipe once.

Thinks + has source access?

Brain with eyes.
Review output like a junior's work.

Thinks + no source access?

Blindfolded brain.
Be most careful here.
